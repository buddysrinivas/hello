Fatal collision at Woodville

#1 | Posted: Yesterday, 18:45 | Edited by: tkx211

One man has died and five people have been injured in a two-car collision at Woodville.
At about 2.15am today a red Ford sedan and a silver Toyota sedan were travelling in opposite directions on Torrens Road, when they collided near the intersection of Torrens Road and Fletcher Street.
Sadly, a 24-year-old man from Woodville North, who was a passenger in the Ford, died enroute to hospital.
Five other people involved in the collision were taken to the QEH for treatment for minor injuries.
A 42-year-old man from Woodville North has been arrested and charged with causing death by dangerous driving.
He will appear in court at a later date.
The man's death takes the state's road toll to 91, compared with 98 at the same time last year.
